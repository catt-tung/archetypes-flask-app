Add a Story

Here you can add a story about an encounter with a survival archetype and how you dealt with it. In sharing your story, feel free to consider the following to help prompt you:

  • When/how did I realize this was an encounter with this specific survival archetype?
  • What was the choice I was faced with making?
  • What was the lesson behind the choice I was called to make?
  • What choice did I make, and how do I feel about it?
  • What was the alternative choice, and how would I have felt about that?
  • What did I learn about myself as a result of this experience/encounter?
  • What did I learn about this archetype as a result of this experience/encounter?

Remember, the intention is to help yourself gain clarity through the writing process, as well as help others to recognize the archetype in their own lives.
